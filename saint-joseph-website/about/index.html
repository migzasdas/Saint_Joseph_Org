About Saint Joseph Choir

Saint Joseph Choir is a community-centered organization promoting service, responsibility, and consistent support for families and youth through structured programs and volunteer leadership.

History

Where we started

This Organization/Group started in 1973 as a small group of volunteers who wanted to serve and give back to the Lord. Years come and the Group grew larger, It begins to do outreach programs and contribution to the community.

Nature (Para saan?)

What we do

This Group was created to serve and express their musicality to worship and praise the Almighty God in the local church San Jose De Balintawak. The group also does outreach programs to help the community and to those who are in need. They also do some activities to promote the group and to encourage people to join and be part of the group.

Mission

Our mission

Our mission is to serve the Lord and the community through our musical talents and outreach programs. We aim to foster a sense of community, responsibility, and service among our members and those we serve.
